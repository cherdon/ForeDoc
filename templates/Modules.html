    Four Modules (1.0.1)


    The Four Modules helps to guide you on how to use the simulation program, inclusive of world generation and scenario simulation.
    You may use it as a reference, or simply read it from the start to the end.

    If this is the first time you are using the simulation program, take a look at the introductory documentation on Four Set-up, and see the Four Tutorials.
